p53 datasets

To further demonstrate the applicative power of SCpre-seq, we applied it to a disease-related protein p53 (GEO accession number GSE138852) containing 13,214 single nuclei collected from six AD and six control brains.

AD case datasets can be downloaded from GEO databases with accession numbers of GSE138852 (AD case).
